Susan MacIntosh's Art Store

Susan is an artsy person with a Bachelor's Degree in Fine Arts from the University of Waterloo. She lives in Calgary, Alberta, where she works as a full time artist. She is inspired by the natural beauty of Alberta, including mountains, horizons, and wildflowers. A life-long artisit, she has been represented by a couple of galleries (paintings), designed greeting cards, made leather dog collars, and fashioned jewelry. After 40 years of artistic wandering, Susan has decided that she is in fact a painter.
